Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

MAST is a financial commitment and accountability tool. You put real money behind your goals, and you get it back when you follow through. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and what control you have over it. We aim to keep things straightforward.

Who We Are

MAST is operated by an independent developer under the name "MAST." We are not a large corporation. We build this tool because we believe skin-in-the-game accountability works, and we treat your data with the care we'd want for our own.

What Data We Collect

When you use MAST, we may collect the following information:

  • Name and email address — provided during setup so we can identify your account and communicate with you.
  • Wallet address — your blockchain wallet address on the Base network, used to manage your escrow funds and commitment contracts.
  • Commitment history — the goals you set, their status, amounts staked, and outcomes. This is the core of how the service works.
  • Profile preferences — information you share with your AI agent (like your name, motivations, and personal mantra) to personalize your experience.

Some commitment data is stored on the Base blockchain and is publicly visible by nature of how blockchains work. On-chain data includes wallet addresses, commitment amounts, and transaction records.

What We Don't Collect

We do not store your credit card details or payment information. When you fund your account, the payment is processed entirely by Coinbase through their Onramp service, which converts your payment into USDC on the Base network. Coinbase handles all payment processing and is subject to their own privacy policy. We never see or store your card number, billing address, or bank details.

How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for these purposes:

  • Operating the service — managing your commitments, processing goal completions, and returning funds when you succeed.
  • Personalizing your experience — using your profile preferences to tailor commitment pages and AI agent interactions to you.
  • Communicating with you — sending updates about your commitments, such as reminders or completion confirmations.
  • Improving the service — understanding how the tool is used so we can make it better.

We Don't Sell Your Data

Your data is not for sale. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing or any other purpose. Your commitment history and profile information exist solely to power the service you signed up for.

Third-Party Services

MAST relies on a small number of third-party services:

  • Coinbase Onramp — processes credit card payments and converts them to USDC. Coinbase collects payment and identity information under their own privacy policy.
  • Base network (blockchain) — commitment transactions are recorded on-chain. On-chain data is public and immutable.

We do not use analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media tracking tools.

Cookies

We don't use cookies. MAST's pages are static and do not set or read browser cookies. There are no tracking pixels, fingerprinting scripts, or hidden identifiers.

Data Retention

We retain your account data and commitment history for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal data from our systems within 30 days. Commitment data that has been recorded on the Base blockchain cannot be deleted, as blockchain records are immutable by design.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Export your data — receive your commitment history and profile data in a portable format.
  • Delete your data — request that we remove your personal information from our systems. Note that on-chain transaction data cannot be removed from the blockchain.
  • Correct your data — update or fix any inaccurate personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below. We'll respond within 30 days.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and limiting access to personal information. Smart contract funds are secured by the Base blockchain. That said, no system is perfectly secure, and we encourage you to protect your own wallet credentials.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "last updated" date at the top. If we make significant changes, we'll make reasonable efforts to notify you.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or your data, reach out to us at privacy@mast.app.
